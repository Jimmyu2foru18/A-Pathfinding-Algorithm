Testing and Validation

Testing Strategy

To ensure the A* algorithm is robust, we employ a multi-layered testing strategy:

  • Unit Tests: Validate individual functions (e.g., heuristic calculation, neighbor generation).
  • Integration Tests: Ensure the algorithm correctly interacts with the visualizer and grid data.
  • Visual Validation: Manually confirm the visualizer correctly reflects algorithm states (open, closed, path, obstacle).

Validating Heuristics

A critical step is validating that heuristic functions are admissible (never overestimate the cost). We do this by comparing results with known simple paths.
