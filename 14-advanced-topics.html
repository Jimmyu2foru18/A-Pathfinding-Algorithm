Advanced Pathfinding

Going Beyond Basic A*

For large-scale, dynamic, or real-time environments, standard A* may not be sufficient. Advanced approaches include:

  • Hierarchical A* (HPA*): Breaking a massive grid into smaller clusters, pathfinding between clusters first, then within clusters.
  • Jump Point Search (JPS): An optimization for uniform-cost grids that "jumps" over nodes that do not change the path direction.
  • Bidirectional A*: Running two concurrent searches (from start and goal) to reduce the search space significantly.
